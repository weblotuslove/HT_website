About The Healing Tigers

Back when tigers used to smoke, a bunch of tigers were awakened by a divine power. They had the healing powers and lived in a healing camp.

Healing Tigers are the navigators of our adventures on earth. They are like divine life coaches, but never interfere your life without your invitation due to the law of Free Will.

Join us and own your tigers, reconnect with them, to be the transmitters of energy and love.

The Rarity

In the middle of healing camp, there are five ancient trees with healing powers. Each tree has its own tribe of tigers to be taken care of. Different tigers have different healing powers.

Yellow Tiger the strongest and heaviest of all the tiger species, is the symbol of Power of Strength. White Tiger also known as snow tiger, is one of the four divine animals in the ancient mythology, symbolizing the power of compassion. Blue Tiger also known as the Maltese tiger, is one of the rarest of the colors, symbolizing the power of manifestation. Black Tiger with distinctive dark stripe pattern, represents the Power of Focus. Red Tiger also known as Puma, Mountain Lion, is the symbol of Power of Instinct.
